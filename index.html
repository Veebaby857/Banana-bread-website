Banana bread recipe

The most simplest ingredients on the counter

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • Sugar
  • eggs
  • Whole milk
  • Melted butter
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350f for 10mins
  2. Mash bananas and mix all the wet ingredients
  3. Sift the dry ingredients together
  4. Combine the dry ingredients to the wet
  5. Add your preferred toppings
