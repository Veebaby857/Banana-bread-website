Banana Bread Recipe 🍞

The simplest and most delicious homemade banana bread 🍌

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Whole milk
  • Melted butter
  • Flour
  • Baking powder

Watch how to make it 🎥

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F for 10 minutes
  2. Mash bananas and mix all wet ingredients
  3. Sift the dry ingredients together
  4. Combine dry and wet ingredients
  5. Add your preferred toppings
